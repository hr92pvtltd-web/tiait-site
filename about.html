About Us

A modern institute for artificial intelligence, technology and innovation.

Tarar Institute of AI & Technology (TIAIT) is a modern higher education institution established to prepare the next generation of technology leaders, legal professionals, entrepreneurs and innovators.

Overview

Who we are

The Institute is committed to delivering world class education, practical skills, research excellence and industry engagement to meet the evolving demands of the digital economy.

TIAIT combines academic excellence with industry driven learning, ensuring graduates possess both theoretical knowledge and practical competencies. Through strategic partnerships with industry, government and international institutions, the Institute aims to become a regional hub for Artificial Intelligence, Computing, Business, Law and emerging technologies.

The Institute also serves as a centre for professional certifications, workforce development, technology consulting and innovation through its software development division and executive learning centre.
