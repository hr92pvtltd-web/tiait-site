Program Overview

The BS Accounting & Finance program at Tarar Institute of AI & Technology prepares students for the finance careers of the digital age. It blends rigorous training in financial and management accounting, corporate finance, taxation and audit with modern skills in business analytics, financial technology and governance — the combination that today's banks, firms and corporations are looking for.

The curriculum is designed to support globally recognised professional pathways, including ACCA, CFA and CA, so students can build toward professional qualifications alongside their degree. With practical case work, ERP and analytics tools, an industrial internship and a final year project, graduates leave financially literate, analytically sharp and ready for the workplace.

Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply? Applicants who have completed 12 years of education (or will do so in the current admission session) are eligible, subject to the following:

Intermediate (Commerce, ICS, Pre-Engineering or any group) Minimum 45% marks A-Levels / foreign qualifications with IBCC equivalence

Per HEC rules, candidates with qualifications other than SSC/HSSC may be required to obtain an IBCC equivalence certificate.

Admission Test & Selection

Admission is based on academic record, an admission test and an interview. The test assesses quantitative reasoning, basic English and general knowledge, and is held at the TIAIT campus. Final merit combines your previous academic results with test performance. Our admissions team will guide you through test dates and preparation.

Fee Structure

TIAIT offers competitive, transparent fees with no hidden charges and flexible, semester-wise installment plans. Merit and need-based scholarships are available for deserving students. For the current per-semester fee for BS Accounting & Finance and details of financial support, please contact the admissions office.
