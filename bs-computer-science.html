Program Overview

The BS Computer Science program at Tarar Institute of AI & Technology is designed to turn curious problem-solvers into confident, industry-ready computer scientists. Over four years, students build a rigorous foundation in programming, algorithms, mathematics and systems, then progress into advanced areas such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cloud computing and cybersecurity.

Our curriculum follows a modern, HEC-aligned structure and is delivered by experienced academics and industry practitioners. Learning is hands-on: students work in dedicated computing and AI laboratories, collaborate on real projects, earn professional certifications and complete an industrial internship and a substantial final year project. Graduates leave able to design, build and deploy intelligent software that solves real-world problems — and ready to compete for careers in Pakistan and abroad.

Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply? Applicants who have completed 12 years of education (or will do so in the current admission session) are eligible to apply, subject to the following:

Intermediate (Pre-Engineering / ICS) or equivalent Minimum 50% marks Mathematics required at intermediate level A-Levels / foreign qualifications with IBCC equivalence

Per HEC rules, candidates with qualifications other than SSC/HSSC may be required to obtain an equivalence certificate from IBCC, Islamabad.

Admission Test & Selection

Admission is based on academic record, an admission test and an interview. The admission test assesses analytical reasoning, mathematics and basic English, and is held at the TIAIT campus. Final merit is determined by a combination of your previous academic results and test performance. Our admissions team will guide you through test dates and preparation.

Fee Structure

TIAIT offers competitive, transparent fees with no hidden charges and flexible, semester-wise installment plans. Merit and need-based scholarships are available for deserving students. For the current per-semester fee for BS Computer Science and details of available financial support, please contact the admissions office.
