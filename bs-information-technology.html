Program Overview

The BS Information Technology program at Tarar Institute of AI & Technology is an applied degree for students who want to build, secure and manage the technology that organisations depend on. It develops practical expertise across networks, systems administration, databases, cloud computing, cybersecurity and enterprise systems, with a strong emphasis on real-world implementation.

Learning is hands-on and lab-based. Students configure networks, deploy cloud services, manage databases and secure systems, while also developing the project-management and communication skills that IT professionals need. With industrial internships, professional certifications and a final year project, graduates are ready to plan, deploy and run the IT that keeps modern businesses moving.

Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply? Applicants who have completed 12 years of education (or will do so in the current admission session) are eligible, subject to the following:

Intermediate (any group, ICS/Pre-Engineering preferred) Minimum 45% marks A-Levels / foreign qualifications with IBCC equivalence

Per HEC rules, candidates with qualifications other than SSC/HSSC may be required to obtain an IBCC equivalence certificate.

Admission Test & Selection

Admission is based on academic record, an admission test and an interview. The test assesses analytical reasoning, basic mathematics and English, and is held at the TIAIT campus. Final merit combines your previous academic results with test performance. Our admissions team will guide you through test dates and preparation.

Fee Structure

TIAIT offers competitive, transparent fees with no hidden charges and flexible, semester-wise installment plans. Merit and need-based scholarships are available for deserving students. For the current per-semester fee for BS Information Technology and details of financial support, please contact the admissions office.
