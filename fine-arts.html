School of Fine Arts & Visual Communication

Creativity, craft and visual storytelling.

From traditional painting and calligraphy to digital illustration, animation and interior design — a school where artistic craft meets modern visual communication, and every student graduates with a professional portfolio.

Overview

Studio-based, industry-ready

Our approach is hands-on and project-driven. Students learn by making — developing technical skill, creative confidence and a body of work that prepares them for careers in design, media and the creative industries, or for freelance practice.
