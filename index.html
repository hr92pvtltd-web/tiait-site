Welcome to TIAIT

World class education, built for the digital economy

Tarar Institute of AI & Technology (TIAIT) is committed to delivering world class education, practical skills, research excellence and industry engagement to meet the evolving demands of the digital economy.

TIAIT combines academic excellence with industry driven learning, ensuring graduates possess both theoretical knowledge and practical competencies. Through strategic partnerships with industry, government and international institutions, the Institute aims to become a regional hub for Artificial Intelligence, Computing, Business, Law and emerging technologies.
