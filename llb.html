Program Overview

The LLB program at Tarar Institute of AI & Technology offers a modern, well-rounded legal education firmly grounded in the Pakistani legal system. Students study the core areas of law — constitutional, contract, criminal, civil procedure, corporate and commercial law — alongside emerging fields such as cyber law and intellectual property, developing both breadth of knowledge and depth of analysis.

The program deliberately balances legal theory with practical skill. Through moot courts, legal research, drafting, legal clinics and internships, students learn to argue, advise and advocate. Our aim is to produce competent, ethical legal professionals prepared for the courtroom, the boardroom and public service.

Eligibility Criteria

Who can apply? Applicants who have completed 12 years of education (or will do so in the current admission session) are eligible, subject to the following:

Intermediate (any group) or equivalent Minimum 45% marks Law Admission Test (LAT) / entry test where applicable A-Levels / foreign qualifications with IBCC equivalence

Admission to law programs is subject to the rules and requirements of the relevant regulatory authorities. Our admissions team will advise on the current requirements.

Admission Test & Selection

Admission is based on academic record, the relevant law admission test and an interview. The assessment evaluates analytical reasoning, comprehension and general knowledge. Final merit combines your previous academic results with test performance. Our admissions team will guide you through test dates, eligibility and preparation.

Fee Structure

TIAIT offers competitive, transparent fees with no hidden charges and flexible, semester-wise installment plans. Merit and need-based scholarships are available for deserving students. For the current per-semester fee for LLB and details of financial support, please contact the admissions office.
