Research & Innovation

Research that solves real-world challenges.

From artificial intelligence and robotics to FinTech, health informatics and climate technologies, research at TIAIT is applied, collaborative and commercially aware — connecting the classroom to the problems that matter.

Our Approach

Applied research with impact

We focus on research that creates real value — for industry, for communities and for our students, who work on live projects from their undergraduate years onward. Our labs and innovation spaces turn ideas into prototypes, products and ventures.
