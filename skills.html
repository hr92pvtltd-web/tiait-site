Skills Development Centre

Employable skills, built with government and industry.

TIAIT partners with the Punjab Skills Development Fund (PSDF) and the National Vocational & Technical Training Commission (NAVTTC) to deliver funded, job-focused training that helps young people in Lahore earn, freelance and build careers in the digital economy.

Overview

Learn a skill. Earn a living.

Our short courses are practical, hands-on and designed with employers — so you finish with skills the market actually pays for. Eligible trainees can study on government-funded programs at little or no cost.
